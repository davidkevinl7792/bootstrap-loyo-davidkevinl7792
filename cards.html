Positioning

Setter. This is where the setter stands. They stand in the front middle to have more vary of where they can aim the ball to which spiker they want.

Libero. This is where the libero stands. They stand in the back middle to be able to guard any ball coming close to the center.

Left Wing Spiker. This is where the left wing spiker stands. They stand in the back left to help the libero and prepare to jump and spike the ball.

Right Wing Spiker. This is where the right wing spiker stands. They stand in the back right to help the libero and prepare to jump and spike the ball.

Left Hitter. This is where the left hitter stands. They stand in the front left so if any ball falls short they can recieve it.

Right Hitter This is where the right hitter stands. They stand in the front right so if any ball falls short they can recieve it.
